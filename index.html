services

Customer Focused

We will be providing a team of technical and business specialists, providing customer-oriented solutions, with an architecture that keeps this in mind at all times, with top class representatives and developers. Our entire strategy is aligned with customer expectations of what the new technology can provide. All our team are experienced in business and understand customer needs and requirements.

Secure Architecture

Blockchain technology uses the latest advances in cryptography to provide a secure platform for all applications, either in a private or public chain.

The Hyperledger solution provides the very latest tools and security terchnology to support distributed applications.

Open Source

Our code is open source, which is a key philosophy of ours, and we encourage all developers to visit our github repository to contribute to the project.

Scalable Solution

We intend to provide a partially decentralized but hugely scalable architecture as a stepping stone to full decentralization. Scalable components are being built for:

  • Blockchain
  • Data Storage
  • Messaging
We also provide components enabling Data Analytics, into the data store, which has been selected with this in mind.

ISO/W3C/GBA Compliant

As soon as international standards for Blockchain are published we intend to comply to those standards at an early a stage as possible.
